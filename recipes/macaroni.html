Macaroni

Description

This is a good pasta that takes about 4.5 hours time to make

Ingredients for 8 servings

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • ¼ cup sweet relish
  • ½ cup finely chopped green bell pepper
  • ½ cup onion, finely chopped
  • ¼ cup finely chopped carrot
  • 1 hard-boiled egg, chopped

Dressings

  • ¼ cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip®)
  • ¼ cup reduced-fat sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons low-fat milk
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain well; rinse with cold water and drain again.
  2. Reserve 2 tablespoons relish juice; place the rest in a large bowl with onion, bell pepper, carrot, and egg.
  3. Whisk creamy salad dressing into reserved relish juice. Mix in sour cream, milk, sugar, dill, salt, and pepper. Fold gently into the macaroni mixture. Refrigerate to let flavors blend, 4 to 24 hours.
